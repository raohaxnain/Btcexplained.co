About Us

Established at the dawn of 2022, BTC Explained exists to be a trusted source of financial education for everyday Joes and Janes who want to learn how to invest in Bitcoin with confidence. We’re not some startup Bitcoin company that can’t put their money where their mouth is. We’re also not here to tell you to fund some crazy goal and pretend you’ll be a millionaire by next Tuesday. We are here to give you hope, proving that there IS a better form of money that can’t be manipulated (and isn’t rigged to make the rich richer).

Lucas Velaquez
CEO Btcexplained

Why Us?

Well, we know trust isn’t a given – it’s earned. That’s why we use our years of experience, our passion for education, and – most importantly – our honesty to verify what we can do, not just tell you to “trust us”. Enough phony get-rich-quick schemes that leave your pockets emptier than when you started. The more people join our community, the more deceptive schemes will vamoose.

Here Are a Few of Our Favorite Things

Simplicity

Bitcoin shouldn’t be cryptic. Throughout our 6+ years of Bitcoin experience, we’ve learned that it’s not as complicated as many people make it out to be. Our users are like family to us, so we care about seeing you succeed – and we do that by keeping things simple. When you join the BTC Explained fam, you’re one of us – and we’ll always be in your corner rooting for you.

Accessibility

Bitcoin investing is NOT something that’s reserved for a select few wealthy geniuses with rocket science degrees. Bitcoin is for everyone, and we believe that ANYONE can learn, understand, and succeed in Bitcoin. All you need is to get schooled. Just ask the students we’ve already spent time with. We hope to see you in class soon too.

Community

Without sounding cliché, community really is a powerful thing – and a little help can make all the difference. Instead of Googling endlessly and still scratching your head whenever you read about Bitcoin, we’re making it easier than ever to learn everything about everything Bitcoin – surrounded by likeminded folk who are on the same learning journey as you.

Telling it Like it Is

Good news; you are born to do more than work, pay bills, and die. With the right knowledge from Bitcoin insiders (hint: that’s us) you can use your financial education to explore the Bitcoin realm and move a little closer to financial freedom every single day. We talk facts, no strings attached, giving you an easy way to learn about Bitcoin, at home, in your PJs.

If knowledge is power, then Bitcoin education is your Mjollnir. Get the upper hand. Join the global community that wants to see you succeed no matter what.

Remember: BITCOIN. IS. FOR. ALL. You ready?
